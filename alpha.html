Why Fovey exists

Fovey was built for makers who want the clarity of vector tools without the noise. It blends a moodboard canvas with precise editing so concepting and execution stay in one place.

The alpha is focused on speed, feedback, and workflows that feel native to creative thinking. We want every action to feel predictable, fast, and purposeful.

  • Lightweight and offline-first
  • Focused on real workflows
  • Fast interactions at any scale
